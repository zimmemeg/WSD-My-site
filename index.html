ZGMF-X19A Infinite Justice

The ZGMF-X19A Infinite Justice was developed by the Clyne Faction and was first deployed in C.E.74. It is very similar to its predecessor, the ZGMF-X09A Justice Gundam.

Fun Facts

  1. The Infinite Justice is the only Gundam of Athrun Zala's to survive
  2. It was originally to be called the "Knight Justice"
  3. Infinite Justice is the former name of Operation:Enduring Freedom, part of the US War on Terror.
  4. "Diciannove" which is printed under the model number is Italian for "19"
